Future Moab Trips

Canyonlands Adventure

Explore the majestic Canyonlands National Park from a unique perspective as you raft through its stunning river canyons. This trip offers a mix of thrilling Class III-IV rapids and calm waters, perfect for soaking in the dramatic landscapes and geological wonders of Moab.

Prepare for an epic adventure through Cataract Canyon, known for its challenging and exhilarating Class III-V rapids. This trip is ideal for experienced rafters seeking an adrenaline-packed experience, with the added bonus of breathtaking views and rich history along the way.
