At Dry Oar Rafting, our customers' satisfaction is our top priority. Our expert guides ensure unforgettable adventures, blending thrilling rapids, stunning scenery, and top-notch safety. History

Founded in 2011, Dry Oar Rafting started as a passion project by a group of outdoor enthusiasts with a love for white water rafting. What began as a small outfit grew rapidly, driven by a commitment to providing thrilling yet safe adventures on the river.

Over the years, we've become a recognized name in the industry, known for our expert guides, top-notch equipment, and unforgettable experiences. Today, Dry Oar Rafting continues to lead the way in white water rafting, delivering excitement and joy to all who seek adventure on the water.
